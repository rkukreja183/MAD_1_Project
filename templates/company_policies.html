Company Policies

## User Responsibility
- Users must adhere to community standards and guidelines.
- Users are encouraged to report any content violating terms of service or community guidelines.

## Content Eligibility
Content flagged for review includes, but is not limited to, explicit/offensive material, copyright infringement, hate speech, and legal violations.

## Flagging Process
- Admins have the authority to flag content, songs, lyrics.
- Flags can be submitted through the app's reporting feature with specified reasons.

## Review Process
- Flagged content will be promptly reviewed by the admin team.
- Admins will follow a fair and unbiased evaluation process.

## Actions Taken
- Upon review, content may be removed, edited, or deemed acceptable based on app guidelines.
- Repeated violations may result in warnings, suspensions, or account termination.

## Creator Accountability
- Creators are responsible for the content they upload.
- Repeated violations may lead to account suspension or removal.

## Privacy Protection
- The app prioritizes user privacy and sensitive information during the review process.

## Continuous Improvement
- Policies will be regularly updated based on user feedback, industry standards, and legal requirements.

## Communication
- Users will be informed of flagged content outcomes within a reasonable time frame.
